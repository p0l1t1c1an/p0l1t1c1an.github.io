Why I Chose To Study Cybersecurity

I would say that my vast interest in the security field comes from my interest in well ... computers, obviously. I still remember being a youngin' like 6-7 and my family giving me their old phones. I would tear those open and look at all the components, then try to put them back together with a 99% failure rate. Then, the cellular companies starting buying back old phone and killing one my main joys as a kid. It wasn't until middle school that I started getting into programming with vex controllers in C++. And in high school, I found interests lasting to this day with using FOSS and Unix-like OSes or low level programming in C (and now Rust). I think cybersecurity is a way for me to learn more about these systems that I took for granted and couldn't even start to explain growing up. Now, I can recognize how naive I was and that I lacked a lot of understanding. And while I would love to learn everything possible about security, cloud computing, ..., I also recognize that's impossible for me alone and should try work with different minded individuals to fill each others gaps in knowledge.
