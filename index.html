Discovering My Interests

When I was child, I would take my family's old laptops and cell phones and dissect them trying to learn more about them. From there, I got interested in circuits and started working with Vex robotics, Arduinos, and Raspberry Pis learning to program in C++ and Python. Then on, I discovered more interesting things like Unix operating systems and low level programming in C. To this day, I am still finding new things to learn and take interest in from low level ideas of networking and wireless communications to high level ideas of web development and setting my own NAS and backup system. I never want to stop learning.
